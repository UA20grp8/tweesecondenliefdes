Installation kaleidoscope

From the collective passage space you unexpectedly enter a 'different world' the moment you just expect to leave the tunnel. You enter this as an individual because the passage to it narrows. The new space in which you find yourself shows a false-looking reflection of the reality that puts the world and your position in it into perspective. Because your expectations are broken several times, you then also question this. Ultimately, you yourself become part of the reflection that the people behind you get to see.
